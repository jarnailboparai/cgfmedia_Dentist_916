About Us


Dr. David Ritholtz

Dr. David Ritholtz founded and has directed Premier Dental Associates of Lower Manhattan since its inception in 1996. He has been practicing general and cosmetic dentistry in the Wall Street area for over twenty five years. Dr. Ritholtz graduated from Yeshiva College with a Bachelor of Arts degree in Biology. He received his Doctor of Dental Surgery degree from the New York University College of Dentistry. He is a member of the American Dental Association and the First District Dental Society of the State of New York.

Dr. Ritholtz resides on Long Island with his family. He loves all sports and is an avid golfer.

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

Dr. Caroline Quiong

Dr. Caroline Quiong graduated cum laude from the University of Pennsylvania with a Bachelor of Arts degree in Biology. Dr. David Galler

Dr. Galler is the leading Invisalign general dentist in lower Manhattan. He is ranked among the top Invisalign dentists in not only New York but across the country as well. Dr. Galler received his dental degree from the University of Pennsylvania and is a member of Omicron Kappa Upsilon- an elite dental honor society.

Originally from Long Island, he moved back to New York in 2003. He has proudly transformed hundreds of smiles with Invisalign. He is among the select few GP ELITE PREMIER PROVIDERS of Invisalign in the country. He is in the top 1% of Invisalign practioners in North America.

His unique IPR technique, now called “GST” (Galler Spacing Technique), has now been published and will be a part of Orthodontic textbooks in the years to come.

Dr. Galler has conducted Invisalign Study Clubs around the country, and was a featured speaker at the 2008 and 2009 Invisalign GP National Summit in Las Vegas. Several of his cases are featured on the Invisalign Website, and one of his recent multi-discipline,x Invisalign/implant/prosthetic/esthetic cases was published by the New York State Dental Journal.

Dr. Rebecca Bae

Dr. Rebecca Bae graduated from New York University College of Dentistry. She was enrolled in the accelerated 7-year BADDS Program at NYU. Dr. Bae completed her general practice residency at Hackensack University Medical Center in Hackensack, NJ. Upon completion of her residency program, she was asked to return as an attending faculty at the hospital.

She has received Associate Fellowship Certification through the World Clinical Laser Institute. Dr. Bae is a member of the American Dental Association, Academy of General Dentistry, New York State Dental Association, and the New York County Dental Association.

She is currently an attending medical staff at Hackensack University Medical Center in NJ where she teaches and oversees dental residents. Dr. Bae actively practices all areas of dentistry as a general dentist in NY and NJ.

Dr. Neer Even Hen
(Periodontist- Gum Specialist)

Dr. Neer Even-Hen is one of the few laser certified periodontists in Manhattan. He utilizes laser technology in both cosmetic gum procedures and root coverage procedures to improve smiles and correct gum recession defects. Dr. Even-Hen treats periodontal disease which is diseases of the gum and surrounding bone that may cause pain, bleeding,x bad breath, tooth mobility and even tooth loss. Dr. Even-Hen utilizes the latestx in laser technology and periodontics to enhance the smiles of patients and treat periodontal disease while keeping a patient’s comfort as a primary focus.

“I believe in minimal discomfort, less invasive procedures and utilizing the latest technology to provide the very best care for my patients.”

Dr. Even-Hen grew up in Montreal, Canada and now resides in Edison, NJ with his wife and three daughters. He graduated cum laude from Yeshiva University with a Bachelor of Arts degree. Dr. Even-Hen received his dental degree in 2000 from the University of Pennsylvania School of Dental Medicine. He earned his specialist certificate in Periodontics from the University of Rochester Eastman Dental Center in 2003. He recently completed his Associate Fellowship training in Laser Dentistry and The Masters Course in Advanced Laser Assisted Periodontics.
